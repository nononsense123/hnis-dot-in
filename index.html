FAQs

What services do we offer?

We provide comprehensive financial planning and investment advisory services, including:

- Mutual funds
- Fixed income products like FDs
- Portfolio Management Services (PMS)
- Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs)
- Insurance solutions

And any new financial products introduced by regulators in the future

Who do we typically work with?

We primarily work with high-net-worth individuals (HNIs) and emerging affluent investors who are looking for structured financial planning and a long-term wealth creation strategy.

I’ve heard of these investment products before. What makes working with you different?

Financial success isn’t about chasing the latest trends—it’s about sticking to a structured plan and executing it consistently. While our approach may seem simple, it’s something 99.99% of investors fail to do. We help you stay disciplined, avoid distractions, and make informed financial decisions that actually build and protect wealth over time.

 Do you provide stock tips or trading calls?

No, and we discourage all forms of speculation in the stock market. Capital preservation is the first and foremost priority in our approach.

What are our qualifications?

We are a team of experienced financial advisors with deep expertise in investment management. Our lead advisor is an actuary-turned-financial expert with 5 years of experience in both India and Canada and holds the following NISM certifications:

NISM-Series-XA: Investment Adviser Certification Examination (Level 1)
NISM-Series-XB: Investment Adviser Certification Exam (Level 2)
NISM-Series-VA: Mutual Fund Distributors Certification Exam
NISM-Series-XXI-A: PMS Distributors Certification Exam
NISM-Series-XIX-A: AIF Distributors Certification Exam (Category 1 and 2)
NISM-Series-XIX-B: AIF Distributors Certification Exam (Category 3)

If I want to invest, do I send you the money and let you decide where it goes?

No, we follow SEBI guidelines, and your funds are always invested in the chosen financial product (e.g., mutual funds, PMS, AIFs) directly from your bank account. You retain full control over your investments at all times.
